Market Analysis and Modeling of Train Ticket Distribution Business on Ctrip.com

  • Analyzed the financial statement data of related businesses in tourism industry, and evaluated the feasibility and development potential of developing this business in Ctrip.
  • Designed different questionnaires in terms of target customer groups, analyzed user data with SPSS, and used SAS to complete a visual analysis report of consumption needs and preferences.
  • For existing users, updated the basic business interface model of the original train ticket product interface to optimize the service.
  • For potential users, planted advertisements in video games to increased website and APP views.
